About Us

Great Destinations began as in at home business helping families plan affordable trips to Disney World. We began solely as an Facebook account, where clients message us through with their requests. Our business grew from one person to a team of 10 over the course of 2 years and on August 13th, 2023 this website you are on launched and ever since we have been able to grant over 250 unforgettable trips to our beloved clients.

Who Are We For?

We are for everybody who loves to exlpore and have new experiences, but we target clients who specifically want the most affordable pricing. We love to plan honeymoon trips, family vacations, spring break, destination weddings, you name it we do it all and for an affordable price.
