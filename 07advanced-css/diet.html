Diet

Like us, food and what we eat is very important to puppies, so that is why it is important to feed puppies with a healthy and nutritious diet. First time puppy owners may get overwhelmed with the amount of options of puppy foods, and are not sure what to feed their pups. But it's simple really, all puppies need are foods they are able to chew and swallow, and foods that contain key nutrients.

Key Nutrients for Puppies

  • Protein
  • Fats
  • Calcium
  • Digestable Carbohydrates

What and What Not Foods to Feed Your Pup

Encouraged foods to feed puppies:
Kibble, chicken, beef, canned food, and wet food
Safe foods your puppy can consume:
Apples, greek yogurt, rice, and watermelon
Foods that are toxic to puppies and should never eat:
Chocolate, alcohol, anything with caffeine, onions, and any dairy products
